The first vaccine came from studies carried out by the English physician Edward Jenner. He observed people who were contaminated by milking cows with a disease of cattle and came to the conclusion that these people were immune to smallpox. The disease, called cowpox, resembled human smallpox by the formation of pustules (lesions with pus).The vaccine is an important form of active immunization (when the body itself produces antibodies) and is based on the introduction of the disease-causing agent (attenuated or inactivated) or substances that these agents produce in a person's body in order to stimulate production of antibodies and memory cells by the immune system. Because of the production of antibodies and memory cells, the vaccine ensures that, when the disease-causing agent infects that person's body, he or she is already prepared to respond quickly, even before the symptoms of the disease appear. The vaccine is therefore an important form of disease prevention.

[Letra b]
O torneio interclasse, pois apresenta uma forma de exclusão, pois os menos
habilidosos vão jogar menos tempo em relação aos mais habilidosos, porém
não apresenta nenhum tipo de seleção.

[Letra c]
A Equipe de Futsal masculina e feminina, pois apresenta exclusão, já que os
